## ⚡ About Me

I'm Emmanuel, a creative developer who lives at the intersection of design and code. I'm not satisfied with things that just work, I want them to feel good too: smooth motion, intentional typography, interfaces people actually enjoy using.

Lately that means two things. First, weaving AI into both my creative process and the products I build, from AI-powered UI generation to AI features inside the apps themselves. Second, going deeper into the 3D space, using tools like Three.js and Blender to bring depth and immersion into web experiences that most sites never attempt.

Design and development aren't separate disciplines for me, they're the same craft seen from two angles.
